Understanding Insurance Complaint Decisions

Waiting periods, retrenchment, lapsed policies and non-payment of premiums

Waiting Period Disputes

Most funeral policies include a waiting period before natural death cover begins.

Example:
Waiting period for natural death is 6 months and 6 paid premiums. Accidental death is covered from first premium.
Retrenchment Complaints

Retrenchment cover usually requires involuntary retrenchment and documentary proof.

Lapsed Policy / Non-payment

If premiums are not paid, policy may lapse, but insurers must notify policyholders first.

If you believe your claim was unfairly rejected, file a complaint via the Complaint Form.
